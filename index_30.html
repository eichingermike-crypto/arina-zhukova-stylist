About Me

The story
behind the style

My journey into fashion began with a deep fascination for art and aesthetics. I spent five years studying at an art school, where I developed a strong sense of visual composition, color and creative expression — skills that continue to shape my approach to styling today.

I studied Art History and Styling at the Istituto Marangoni, where I was awarded a scholarship grant in recognition of my creative potential. I also expanded my academic background at Bocconi University, gaining insight into the strategic and business side of the fashion industry.

In addition, I studied Marketing and Creative Communications at Vysoká škola kreativní komunikace in Prague, which allows me to approach styling not only from an aesthetic perspective but also from a strategic one — particularly when creating strong visual concepts for photo shoots, video projects and brand campaigns.

Throughout my career I have collaborated with brands, creative teams and agencies in Paris and Milan, combining industry experience with personal styling.

Today I work with private clients to create wardrobes that feel effortless, confident and truly personal, while continuously developing my expertise through ongoing professional education.
